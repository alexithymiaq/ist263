POKEMON TCG HISTORY & OVERVIEW

Origins (1996-1999)

Pokémon TCG was first published in Japan by Media Factory in 1996, then brought to the U.S. by Wizards of the Coast in 1999. The first expansion was the Base Set featuring the legendary "Base Set Charizard."

Growth & Evolution

Pokémon exploded in popularity during the 1999–2000 craze. It expanded into multiple sets, tournaments, and became a staple in trading card culture.

Why Fans Collect & Play

  • Nostalgia: Many players grew up with Pokémon and still cherish their cards.
  • Artwork: Cards feature unique illustrations by many artists.
  • Value: Some rare cards sell for thousands of dollars.
  • Community: Playing and trading create bonds between fans worldwide.
